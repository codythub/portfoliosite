About Me

Hi, I'm Lanre Malumi, a user interface designer based in Lagos. I am a multidisciplinary designer with a curious mind, I enjoy meeting new people and understand how they are solving problems. I strive to take every opportunity to grow through collaboration, trainings and online resources. Tools i'm highly proficient in include: Wordpress, Adobe Xd, Figma, Adobe Photoshop.

As a relentless problem solver, I am self-aware of the impact design have on people's lives and this is the reason why I always give my best to create a seamless and delightful experience; i'm constantly fascinated with how a creative outlook plays a vital role in life. I'm ready to take on new challenges and opportunity to showcase my abilities.
